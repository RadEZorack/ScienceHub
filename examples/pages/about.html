Understanding Quantum Mechanics

Quantum mechanics is a branch of physics that deals with the behavior of particles at atomic and subatomic scales. It is based on principles that often contradict classical mechanics, such as the idea that particles can exist in multiple states simultaneously.

Key Concepts

The core concepts of quantum mechanics include:

  • Wave-Particle Duality
  • Quantum Superposition
  • Entanglement
  • Heisenberg's Uncertainty Principle
